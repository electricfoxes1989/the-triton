## About Triton
Triton stands for expertise, indispensable information, and fascinating stories about a unique industry. Parent company YATCO (trusted global marketplace for yacht listings) + ACREW (leading platform for professional yacht crew development).

For two decades, Triton has been the voice of the people who keep the yachting industry moving. Go-to source for technical expertise, regulatory information, career advice, and useful services.
