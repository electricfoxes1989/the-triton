# Newsletter Signup Section Redesign

## Changes Made

The newsletter signup banner has been completely redesigned with a premium, professional appearance:

### Visual Design
- **Gradient Background**: Rich gradient from primary navy blue through mid-tone blue to turquoise (#00BCD4)
- **Subtle Pattern**: Decorative SVG pattern overlay at 5% opacity for visual texture
- **Badge Element**: "Stay Informed" badge with frosted glass effect (backdrop-blur)
- **Professional Typography**: Large, bold headline "Never Miss a Story" with improved hierarchy

### Content Updates
- Updated subscriber count to 27,000+ (matching other sections)
- Enhanced copy: "Join 27,000+ industry professionals receiving weekly insights on yachting news, crew life, and maritime careers"
- Added trust indicators below form: "Free weekly newsletter • Unsubscribe anytime • No spam, ever"

### Form Design
- **Frosted Glass Container**: White/10 background with backdrop blur and border
- **Clean Input Field**: White background with proper focus states
- **Prominent CTA Button**: Turquoise (#00BCD4) with hover effects and scale animation
- **Responsive Layout**: Stacks vertically on mobile, horizontal on desktop

### Trust Indicators
- Three-column grid showing key metrics:
  - 27k+ Subscribers
  - Weekly Delivery
  - 20+ Years Publishing
- Separated by vertical borders for visual clarity

## Visual Impact
The new design creates a premium, editorial feel that matches the quality of the Triton brand, with improved visual hierarchy, better use of whitespace, and professional styling that encourages signups.
